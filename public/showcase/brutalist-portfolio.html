A.RAW

A.RAW (Aisha Rawlings) works at the intersection of graphic confrontation and structural critique. Born in Lagos, based in Berlin, trained at ECAL and Central Saint Martins.

The work refuses legibility as a courtesy. If it makes you slightly uncomfortable, it's working.
