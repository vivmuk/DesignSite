Philosophy

Radical subtraction

The Bugatti system removes everything that doesn't serve the object. No gradients. No shadows. No rounded corners that soften intent. What remains is the architecture of speed — vertical lines, cinematic black space, and display type that reads from across a showroom.

Every surface earns its place. Every type size is a decision, not a default. The white page is our Galibier — aerodynamic, purposeful, and ruthlessly fast.

System Elements

Built for monument

01 — Typography

Monumental Display

Weight 900 at 72–180px. Subheads at weight 300 italic. Two weights, maximum contrast. The type IS the design.

02 — Color

Monochrome Only

#000000 on #FFFFFF. One system, one axis. Gray for metadata. Color appears only in photography — never in UI.

03 — Geometry

Zero Radius

Every container, every button, every image frame is a perfect rectangle. Roundness softens. We cut.

04 — Space

Cinematic Pacing

100–140px between sections. Breath is structure. The silence between sections is the design.

05 — Photography

Full-Bleed Only

Images fill their frame edge-to-edge. No rounded corners. No drop shadows. The car IS the composition.

06 — Motion

Fade-Through

Crossfades between states. 600ms ease-in-out. No bounce, no overshoot. A dissolve, like film.
