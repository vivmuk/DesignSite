THE EXPERIENCE

Where darkness reveals light.

Every design choice mirrors the eclipse: dark surfaces that let golden accents corona-bloom into prominence.

🌑

Celestial Dark Base

Near-black backgrounds (#0A0A0E) that simulate totality. Dark surfaces that make every golden accent corona-glow.

✦

Corona Gold Accent

#D4A843 — the warm gold of the solar corona. Used for headlines, CTAs, and the moments that need to shine through darkness.

◉

Eclipse Typography

Playfair Display for dramatic headlines, Inter for readable body copy. A pairing that balances spectacle and clarity.

💫

Radiant Glow Effects

Subtle text-shadow and box-shadow glows that simulate the corona's ethereal light. Illumination without glare.

🌄

Shadow Transitions

State changes inspired by eclipse phases: partial → totality → diamond ring. Animations that mirror nature's drama.

⭐

Star Field Textures

Subtle dot-pattern backgrounds and star-field elements that evoke the midday sky during totality.
