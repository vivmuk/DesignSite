FEATURES

Classic design,
modern craft.

Every component channels decades of design history — then makes it work for the web of today.

01

Editorial Typography

Playfair Display for headings, Inter for body. Serif meets sans in a pairing that echoes broadsheet newspapers and magazine mastheads.

02

Retro Color Palettes

Amber warmth, faded cream, and deep ink tones. Colors drawn from mid-century print design, adapted for screen readability.

03

Border-Heavy Layouts

Generous 2px borders and structured dividers that reference the clean grid systems of 1960s Swiss and editorial design.

04

Nostalgic Motion

Transitions that feel like flipping a page — smooth but deliberate. No bounce, no elastic. Just dignified, intentional movement.

05

Warm Surfaces

Off-white and cream backgrounds that feel like aged paper. No harsh pure white — every surface has warmth, like it's been here for decades.

06

Timeless Patterns

Decorative rules, dots, and dividers that reference classic print design. Never trendy — always tasteful.
