Contrast Ratio

Less is more. Literally.

⬛

Maximum Contrast

Black on white. White on black. No gradients, no half-measures. Every element either is or isn't — like your content deserves.

📐

Stark Typography

Weight and scale do the heavy lifting. 900 vs 300. 4rem vs 0.8rem. The hierarchy is unmistakable because there's nothing else competing.

✂️

Brutal Reduction

Every border, every shadow, every character must earn its place. If it can be removed without losing meaning, it's gone.

🔲

Grid Discipline

Rigid grids. Zero decoration. The structure itself becomes the aesthetic — order that feels intentional, not accidental.

👁️

Pure Readability

No color to hide behind. If the typography can't carry the message alone, the design has already failed.

🕋

Intentional Restraint

Choosing black and white isn't laziness — it's the hardest constraint. When you can't rely on color, every other decision matters more.
