Principles

Built on intention

01

Warmth over whiteness

The paper canvas (#efe7d2) replaces stark white. Every surface carries warmth — like a well-kept book, not a hospital corridor. Tone-on-tone depth, never flat.

02

Serif commands, sans obeys

Playfair Display leads headlines. DM Sans carries the body. Two families, one voice — the serif sets the tone, the sans reads the notes.

03

Collage, not grid

Elements overlap, bleed, and recombine. Asymmetric compositions carry more energy than balanced ones. Break the grid, then rebuild it.

04

Surreal moments

One unexpected element per page. A shifted baseline, an oversized numeral, a color that appears once and disappears. Visual poetry, not decoration.
