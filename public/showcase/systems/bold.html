Principles

Brutal clarity

01

Weight 900

Maximum type weight at every heading level. Display, section, subtitle — all 900. Variation comes from size, not weight.

02

4px Borders

Thick structural borders. No 1px hairlines. Every boundary is a declaration, not an accident. Edges define the page.

03

Zero Radius

Square corners everywhere. The system is geometric, not organic. Right angles feel decisive and absolute.

04

High Contrast

Black on white, or white on black. No gray areas. The accent color appears exactly twice: CTA and kicker.

05

Uppercase

All headings, labels, and CTAs in uppercase. The system doesn't whisper — it announces. Text transforms serve hierarchy.

06

Asymmetric Grid

960px with 24px gutters. Left-weighted layouts. Asymmetry creates tension. Tension creates attention.

In Practice

The Bold system inverts when it needs to. Dark mode isn't a feature — it's the same system, same borders, same weight, on a #111111 surface.
