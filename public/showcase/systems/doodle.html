What you can doodle
✏️

Sketch Components

Buttons, cards, inputs — all drawn with hand-rendered borders and wobbly lines that feel alive.

🖍️

Color Palettes

Bright, playful colors that pop without overwhelming. Sticky-note yellows, highlighter pinks, marker blues.

📐

Grid Systems

Flexible, slightly-offset grids. Imperfect is the point — organized chaos that feels intentional.

✂️

Paper Cut Effects

Torn edges, tape strips, and sticky-note callouts. Real-world metaphors for delightful UIs.

🖌️

Hand-Drawn Icons

SVG icons rendered with imperfect paths. Slightly wobbly, always charming. Like they were doodled just for you.

📝

Annotations

Arrow callouts, margin notes, and scribbled labels. Perfect for onboarding, tooltips, and walkthroughs.
