Philosophy

Brand Statement

Maison represents the intersection of heritage craftsmanship and contemporary design. Every element of our identity speaks to a commitment to excellence, from the carefully chosen typography that evokes classical beauty to the refined color palette that whispers rather than shouts. This is a brand for those who appreciate the subtleties that distinguish the extraordinary from the ordinary.

Standards

Usage Guidelines

Do

Maintain generous whitespace around the logo. Use the gold accent sparingly for maximum impact. Pair serif headings with clean sans-serif captions for elegant contrast.

Don't

Distort or rotate the logo. Use more than three colors in any composition. Apply drop shadows or other effects to logotypes.
